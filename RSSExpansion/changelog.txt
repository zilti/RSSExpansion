==================================================
RSSExpansion 1.3.1-1 for KSP 1.3.1 (2018-02-08)
==================================================

Change Log:

    • Added Principia compatibility to all bodies.
    • Added the asteroid 101955 Bennu, courtesy of KSP forum user KillAshley.
    • Updated all body configs for compatibility with the new KSP and Kopernicus releases.
    • Converted all readable .PNG texture maps into .DDS format.

Bug Fixes:

    • Fixed the inclinations of some bodies.
    • Fixed the SOI ranges of some small bodies, the Jupiter and Saturn moons being under the surface.
    • Fixed the maximum zoom levels in the Tracking Station and the Map View scenes.

Known Issues:

    • The Churyumov-Gerasimenko and Halley comets are currently missing their coma features.
